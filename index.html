Our Team!

Meet the Team - Carolina Rural Arcades was founded by the Stallings Brothers Michael and Cody Stallings. With them being both born in the family with disabilities, Cerebral Palsy. And being Blind. They both had it hard growing up and stayed home most days and found their love for gaming, through the times they noticed some gems being lost to time. So they vowed to preserve everything they could from there precious childhood, thus, Carolina Rural Arcades was born!
